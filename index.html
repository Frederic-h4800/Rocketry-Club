Objective

The purpose of the 8 Globemaster Advanced Rocketry Program is to guide, educate, and expand the young and ever growing generation of space enthused adolescents from 8 Globemaster. The weekly program hopes to inspire and shape the future of low-power model rocketry and strive for goals such as achieving high altitudes and successful recoveries of launch vehicles.
