Globemaster Advanced RP

Objective

The purpose of the 8 Globemaster Advanced Rocketry Program is to guide, educate, and expand the young and ever growing generation of space enthusiasts from 8 Globemaster Squadron. This weekly program hopes to inspire adolescents to develop interest in low-powered rocketry and help them strive for goals such as achieving high altitudes and successful recoveries of launch vehicles.

2019-2020 Rockets

The rockets we will be available for the 2019 - 2020 year for rocketry club are all two-staged rockets. The selections are the Loadstar II, Hyper Bat, and the Navajo AGM rockets.

Loadstar 2

  • Height: 590mm (23")
  • Diameter: 42mm (1.64")
  • Mass: 79 g (2.8 oz.)
  • Motor Mount: 18mm
  • Stages: 2
  • Recovery: Parachute
  • Projected Max Altitude: 825m (1000')
  • Engines: A, B, or C class engines

Hyper Bat
